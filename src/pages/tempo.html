    Largo

    Slowly and broadly

    40 - 60 B.P.M.

    Adagio

    Slowly (but not as slow as largo)

    60 - 75 B.P.M.

    Andante

    At a walking pace

    75 - 105 B.P.M.

    Moderato

    At a moderate pace

    105 - 120 B.P.M.

    Allegro

    Quite fast

    120 - 155 B.P.M.

    Vivace

    Quick and lively

    155 - 175 B.P.M.

    Presto

    Very fast

    175 - 200 B.P.M.

    Tempo Markings

    Tempo markings are placed at the start of the score above the stave

    Tempo Changes

    Terms which indicate a change in tempo during a piece

    Accelerando

    Gradually speeding up

    Ritardando / Rallentando

    Gradually slowing down

    Ritenuto

    A sudden slowing down

    Rubato

    A highly expressive technique where a performer plays with flexible tempo
